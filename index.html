Research Paper

SounDiT

Geo‑contextual Soundscape‑to‑Landscape Generation

We present SounDiT, a method that translates ambient audio into realistic, geo‑aware landscape imagery using advanced diffusion transformers.

Read Paper arXiv User Study

About

SounDiT leverages the power of diffusion transformers to generate realistic landscape imagery from ambient audio recordings. Our method incorporates geographic context to produce spatially-aware visualizations that accurately reflect the acoustic characteristics of the environment.

This work bridges the gap between auditory perception and visual representation, enabling new applications in environmental monitoring, urban planning, and creative media generation.
